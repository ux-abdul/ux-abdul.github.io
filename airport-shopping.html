Airport Shopping

Enhancing the pre-flight shopping experience with personalized recommendations and seamless mobile checkout

Role

Lead UX Designer

Duration

2 Weeks

Platform

iOS, Android

Industry

E-Commerce

Overview

Airport Shopping connects travelers with retail experiences while they wait for their flights, offering personalized product discovery and time-sensitive deals.

The Challenge

Airport travelers have limited time and attention spans. They need to browse, purchase, and arrange delivery quickly while managing tight flight schedules and security concerns.

The Solution

A mobile-first shopping platform that uses flight data to prioritize time-sensitive browsing, offers gate delivery, and integrates with airport maps for quick in-store pickups.

Research & Insights

Key Findings

  • ‚óè 78% of travelers browse their phones while waiting at the gate
  • ‚óè Average dwell time at airports is 90 minutes before domestic flights
  • ‚óè Travelers avoid shopping due to concerns about time and carrying bags

User Pain Points

Time Pressure

Fear of missing boarding calls while shopping

Carrying Luggage

Don't want to carry additional bags through security

Store Location

Difficulty finding specific stores in large airports

Limited Selection

Airport stores often have restricted inventory

User Persona

Susmita Rao

Business Traveler, 34

Susmita is a marketing consultant who travels 2-3 times per month for client meetings. She values efficiency and wants to maximize her limited time at airports. She's tech-savvy and prefers mobile shopping to avoid carrying extra bags through security.

Goals

  • Shop for last-minute gifts and travel essentials without missing her flight
  • Browse duty-free products while waiting at the gate
  • Avoid carrying additional bags through airport security

Pain Points

  • Never knows how much time she actually has to shop before boarding
  • Stores are often far from her gate, making quick purchases difficult
  • Limited product selection in physical airport stores

"I wish I could browse and buy duty-free items from my phone while waiting at the gate, without worrying about missing my boarding call."
