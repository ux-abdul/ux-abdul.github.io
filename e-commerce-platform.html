Case Study

E-Commerce Platform

Redesigning the online shopping experience to increase conversion rates and customer satisfaction through data-driven insights

Role

Lead UX Designer

Duration

10 months

Platform

Web, Mobile

Team

8 members

Overview

A complete redesign of a mid-sized e-commerce platform serving 500K+ monthly users, focusing on improving product discovery and streamlining the checkout process.

The Challenge

The existing platform suffered from high cart abandonment rates (73%), poor product discoverability, and a complex checkout flow that frustrated users. Mobile conversion was particularly low at 1.2%, significantly below industry standards.

The Solution

We reimagined the entire shopping journey with improved navigation, personalized product recommendations, one-click checkout, and a mobile-first design approach that prioritized speed and usability.
