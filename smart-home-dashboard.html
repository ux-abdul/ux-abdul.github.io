Case Study

Smart Home Dashboard

Creating an intuitive control center for smart home automation that simplifies device management and energy monitoring

Role

Lead UX Designer

Duration

8 months

Platform

Web, iOS, Android

Team

6 members

Overview

Smart Home Dashboard provides homeowners with a unified interface to control all their smart devices, monitor energy usage, and automate daily routines.

The Challenge

Most smart home users struggle with fragmented ecosystems, requiring multiple apps to control different devices. This creates confusion, reduces adoption, and prevents users from maximizing the potential of their smart home investments.

The Solution

A comprehensive dashboard that unifies device control, provides intelligent automation suggestions, and offers actionable insights into energy consumption - all through an intuitive, visually appealing interface.
