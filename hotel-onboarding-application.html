Business Impact

Streamlining the user onboarding process enhances efficiency by merging guest information updates with membership enrollment, reducing navigation time for front desk associates. Integrating guest request handling saves time and costs, while the improved search functionality, including arrival date, ensures quicker booking retrieval, significantly boosting overall customer satisfaction and operational productivity.
