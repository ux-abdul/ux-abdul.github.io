Hotel Onboarding App

Streamlining hotel guest check-in and membership enrollment to reduce wait times and improve operational efficiency

Overview

Reimagining the hotel guest experience

A leading hotel chain in the USA was struggling with a complex, time-consuming guest onboarding process that frustrated both front desk staff and guests. The existing system required staff to navigate multiple platforms, resulting in longer wait times and reduced guest satisfaction.

I led the end-to-end redesign of their hotel management application, focusing on streamlining workflows, consolidating features, and creating an intuitive interface that enhanced both employee efficiency and guest experience.

The Solution

Four key improvements that transformed the experience
