Case Study

Hotel Onboarding App

Streamlining hotel guest check-in and membership enrollment to reduce wait times and improve operational efficiency

Role

Lead UX Designer

Timeline

8 weeks

Platform

Salesforce

Industry

Hospitality

Overview

Reimagining the hotel guest experience

A leading hotel chain in the USA was struggling with a complex, time-consuming guest onboarding process that frustrated both front desk staff and guests. The existing system required staff to navigate multiple platforms, resulting in longer wait times and reduced guest satisfaction.

I led the end-to-end redesign of their hotel management application, focusing on streamlining workflows, consolidating features, and creating an intuitive interface that enhanced both employee efficiency and guest experience.

User Personas

Persona 1

Alice Johnson

Front Desk Associate , 32

Experienced hotel staff member managing 50+ daily check-ins at a busy downtown property. Values efficiency and delivering excellent guest service.

A day in their life

  • Handles check-in for 50+ guests daily during peak hours
  • Manages membership enrollments and profile updates
  • Coordinates with housekeeping and maintenance teams for guest requests

Pain points

  • Switching between multiple systems disrupts workflow and slows down check-in process
  • Guests become impatient during lengthy enrollment and data entry procedures

Persona 2

James Smith

Corporate Employee Male , 28

Frequent business traveler who values quick, seamless check-in experiences. Travels 2-3 times monthly and expects consistent service quality.

A day in their life

  • Arrives at hotels late evening after long business meetings
  • Needs quick access to room and amenities without delays
  • Expects membership benefits to be automatically recognized and applied

Pain points

  • Repetitive information requests at each hotel visit despite being a loyalty member
  • Long wait times at front desk during peak check-in hours

Advanced Search

To enhance the user experience and reduce friction in finding guest details, I redesigned the search functionality from a generic keyword-based approach to a context-aware Advanced Search. The new design empowers users to quickly locate bookings using specific filters such as first name, last name, member ID, room number, confirmation number, reservation status, and a custom date range.

This improvement delivers:

Precision ‚Äì Eliminates irrelevant results by narrowing the scope to meaningful attributes.

Speed ‚Äì Reduces time spent scanning large datasets, enabling faster decision-making.

Usability ‚Äì Provides a clear, intuitive interface for complex queries without overwhelming the user.

The result is a streamlined, efficient search experience that supports hotel staff in managing guest onboarding with confidence and ease.
