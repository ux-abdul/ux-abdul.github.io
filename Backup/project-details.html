The Challenge

The Challenge

During peak travel seasons, guests were experiencing check-in wait times exceeding 45 minutes. This bottleneck caused frustration, crowded lobbies, and significantly lower satisfaction scores (CSAT) upon arrival—a critical moment for setting the tone of the stay.

The hotel needed a solution that could offload the administrative burden from front-desk staff while providing a seamless, premium experience for guests.

Complex onboarding flow

Multi-step guest check-in process caused confusion and delays during peak hours

Redundant data entry

Guest information and membership enrollment required separate forms with duplicate fields

Third-party dependency

Guest requests were handled through an external application, creating workflow disruption

Limited search capability

Basic name-only search made it difficult to quickly locate bookings

The Solution

The Solution

We designed a mobile-first web application allowing guests to complete the entire check-in process before arrival. By integrating with the hotel's PMS (Property Management System), guests could verify their identity, authorize payments, and receive a digital room key directly to their device.

Based on research insights, I designed solutions that addressed each major pain point while maintaining system flexibility.

Design Process

Design Process

We adopted a rapid iterative approach, starting with low-fidelity wireframes to validate user flows with actual hotel guests. Key focus areas included:

  • Simplification: Breaking complex forms into digestible steps.
  • Trust: Using clear visual cues for security during ID upload and payment.
  • Accessibility: Ensuring the app was usable for travelers of all ages and abilities.

Outcome

Outcome

By streamlining workflows and consolidating features, the hotel chain reduced operational costs, improved staff productivity, and enhanced guest satisfaction scores. The integrated request management system alone saved an estimated $50,000 annually in third-party software costs while improving response times by 65%.
