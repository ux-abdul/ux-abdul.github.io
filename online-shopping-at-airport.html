Online Shopping from Display Ad at Airport

Designed a seamless checkout experience triggered from an airport display ad, enabling first-time users to move from inspiration to purchase in moments.

The Challenge

The Challenge

Travelers at airports often have significant "dwell time" but limited shopping options near their gates. Carrying physical bags is inconvenient, and finding specific items in a sprawling terminal is stressful.

The challenge was to capture impulse buying intent from digital display ads and convert it into a purchase without forcing the user to download an app or visit a physical store.

Friction-heavy checkout

Traditional mobile web checkouts require too many steps (account creation, address entry) for a traveler in a rush.

Trust & Security

Users are hesitant to enter credit card details on public wifi or unfamiliar sites.

Logistics Anxiety

"Will it get to my gate in time?" is a major blocker for airport delivery services.

Persona

Persona

Samantha Ross

Sales Executive, 34

Frequent flyer who travels weekly for client meetings. She usually arrives at the airport just in time but often has 20-30 minutes of downtime at the gate.

Goals

  • Buy gifts for family without carrying them
  • Use downtime productively
  • Avoid standing in lines at duty-free shops

Frustrations

  • Slow wifi and tedious checkout forms
  • Limited selection at gate kiosks
  • Fear of missing boarding calls while shopping
